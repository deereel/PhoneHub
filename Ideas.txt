About Live GPS Tracking

This is where many people misunderstand what IMEI can do.

You CANNOT
Track live GPS using IMEI.
See a phone's current location from your own software.
Turn on GPS remotely.
Access mobile network location data.
Locate a phone after the SIM card is changed.

Those capabilities require access to carrier infrastructure or the phone manufacturer's ecosystem.

What is possible with a companion app (if the customer opts in)

If you develop a companion Android application and the customer voluntarily installs it and grants the required permissions, you can offer features such as:

Sharing the phone's last known location with the owner.
Sending an alert if the SIM card is changed.
Recording the new SIM number (where platform permissions allow).
Detecting factory reset attempts (with limitations).
Periodically syncing location when the phone is online.
Displaying a message on the lock screen with contact information.

This would be an opt-in security service, not IMEI tracking.

Note: On iPhones, Apple imposes much stricter restrictions, so a third-party app cannot provide the same level of device monitoring.
